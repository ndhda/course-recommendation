What is TOPSIS ?

TOPSIS (Technique for Order Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution) is a method used in MCDM to rank alternatives based on their distance from an ideal solution.

How It Works:

Ideal Solution: Represents the best possible scenario.

Negative Ideal Solution: Represents the worst-case scenario.

Distance Calculation: Alternatives are evaluated based on their geometric distance to both the ideal and negative ideal solutions.

Benefits of MCDM

Simplicity: Easy to understand and implement.

Effectiveness: Provides clear rankings of alternatives.

Flexibility: Can be applied to various decision-making scenarios.

Conclusion

MCDM is a valuable tool for making complex decisions, supporting informed choices through systematic evaluation.
